# Account Deletion and Data Requests

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data. We will delete or de-identify data that is no longer required for legal, security, or compliance reasons.

## Requesting deletion

The app includes an in-app deletion option, use that path first. Otherwise, you can email us using the template below.

[Send email to poss111dev@gmail.com](mailto:poss111dev@gmail.com)

**Email template**

- Subject: `Account deletion request`
- Body: `Include the email/ID used in the app and any necessary verification details we ask for.`
- Send to: `**poss111dev@gmail.com** (replace with your actual support or privacy email).`

## What will be deleted

- Account/profile information and identifiers associated with your account.
- Team information and preferences associated with your account.
- Associated usage data and logs, unless retention is required by law or for security/fraud prevention.

## What may be retained

- Data required to comply with law, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.
- Aggregated or de-identified data that no longer identifies you.

## Timeline

- We acknowledge requests promptly and complete eligible deletions within a reasonable timeframe, subject to verification.

## Revoking permissions and notifications

- You can withdraw optional permissions (location, camera, photos, microphone, notifications) via system settings. Some features may stop working if permissions are revoked.
